Domus

Domus is a sophisticated architectural magazine designed to provide an immersive reading experience. With its minimalist aesthetic and intuitive interface, the platform seamlessly combines editorial content, online purchasing functionality, and the latest architectural news updates.

Domus prioritizes user experience by offering a clean and intuitive interface. Whether users are browsing articles, purchasing the magazine, or exploring architectural news, they can do so effortlessly thanks to the platform's user-friendly design.

Key Features

Online Magazine Purchasing: Domus allows users to conveniently purchase the magazine directly from the website, providing a seamless e-commerce experience.

Architectural News Updates: Stay informed about the latest developments in the architectural world with Domus's curated news section, delivering timely updates to users.

Minimalist Design Language: The minimalist design language of Domus emphasizes clarity and focus, allowing the content to shine without unnecessary distractions.

Nike

The Nike app design project embodies innovation and functionality, offering users a dynamic platform to engage with the Nike brand. From seamless shopping experiences to personalized recommendations, this app redefines the way users interact with athletic apparel and footwear.

The Nike app prioritizes user experience by offering a seamless interface that caters to the diverse needs of athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Whether users are searching for the perfect pair of running shoes or tracking their workout progress, the app provides intuitive navigation and robust functionality to enhance their overall experience.

Key Features

Sleek Product Showcases: The Nike app boasts sleek and intuitive product showcases, allowing users to browse through a curated selection of Nike shoes with ease. Each shoe is presented in a visually appealing manner, showcasing its design, color options, and key features.

Detailed Product Pages: Upon selecting a shoe, users are directed to detailed product pages that provide comprehensive information about the selected item. From sizing options to product specifications and pricing details, users can find everything they need to know before making a purchase decision.

Smooth Navigation: The app offers smooth and intuitive navigation, ensuring that users can effortlessly switch between browsing different shoe categories, exploring product details, and adding items to their cart. The user interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for users to find what they're looking for.

Development of Menu Display

The development project serves as an exercise to demonstrate both coding skills and design concepts. It involves creating a dynamic menu display for a restaurant or food service establishment using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Here's a breakdown of the project:

Coding Practice: The project offers an opportunity to practice JavaScript programming by implementing functionalities such as rendering menu items dynamically and filtering them based on categories. It also involves HTML and CSS coding to structure the webpage and style the menu display.

Design Exploration: While the primary focus is on coding, the project also allows for exploration of design principles. Design choices, such as layout, typography, and color scheme, can be experimented with to create an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly menu display.

Interactive Elements: Interactive elements like filter buttons enhance user engagement and provide a hands-on experience. Users can interact with the menu display to explore different categories of menu items, adding an element of interactivity to the project.
